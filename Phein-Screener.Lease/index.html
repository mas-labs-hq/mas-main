Capabilities

Everything your hiring panel needs.

A focused toolkit built for HR teams that need to move fast without sacrificing rigor. No bloat, no learning curve, no per seat pricing.

Instant candidate ranking

Drop in a job description and a folder of CVs. Phein scores and ranks every candidate against your criteria in seconds, so your shortlist is ready before your coffee cools.

Side by Side Comparison

Select two or more candidates and Phein lays out their skills, experience, and screening scores next to each other, with suggested interview questions tailored to the gaps.

AI Detection Lens

Statistical analysis of vocabulary and structure estimates the likelihood that a CV was generated or assisted by AI. Use it as a conversation prompt during interviews, not a verdict.

Toggles for every dimension

Surface only what you need. Hide age to comply with anti discrimination rules, filter by location, or widen the skills net. All controlled by simple toggles on the screening panel.

Desktop PWA, installable

Install Phein Screener to your desktop or start menu. It launches like a native application, runs full screen, and is gated to PC access only. No phones, no tablets, no exceptions.

Sample data included

New to Phein? A single click loads a representative sample of CVs and a sample job description so you can see the full ranking and comparison flow in action immediately.
